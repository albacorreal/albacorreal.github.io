MSc Projects

Project 01

Design of a MEMS Accelerometer

K Layout · Design & Manufacturing Manager

Designing and managing the fabrication pipeline for a MEMS accelerometer in K Layout. Currently developing an open-source library for MEMS devices, with particular focus on on-chip metrology — including Vernier scale structures for precise dimensional characterisation directly within the die.

Project 02

Heterogeneous Integration of PICs & EICs

Advanced Packaging · ET4391 — Literature Review & Feasibility Analysis

Comprehensive literature review and feasibility analysis of co-integration strategies for Photonic and Electronic ICs. Focused on high-frequency solutions — specifically Thin-Film Lithium Niobate (TFLN) — with a thorough examination of Optical Circuit Switches (OCS) as a use case for next-generation AI datacenter interconnects.

PICs & EICsTFLNOptical Circuit SwitchesAI DatacentersAdvanced Packaging
Project 03

Manufacturing of Perovskite–CIGS Solar Cells

Thin Film Materials · Manufacturing Scalability

Investigating the manufacturing scalability of Perovskite and CIGS tandem thin-film solar cells. Research explores alternatives to ultrasonic spin-coating for conformal deposition at industrial scale, alongside techniques for pinhole effect reduction — a critical yield and reliability challenge in perovskite absorber layers.
