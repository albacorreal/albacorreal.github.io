Heterogeneous Integration of PICs & EICs

Advanced Packaging · ET4391 — Literature Review & Feasibility Analysis

Comprehensive literature review and feasibility analysis of co-integration strategies for Photonic and Electronic ICs. Focused on high-frequency solutions. Project 03 — Ongoing

Manufacturing of Perovskite–CIGS Solar Cells

Thin Film Materials · ME46065 — Manufacturing Scalability

Investigating the manufacturing scalability of Perovskite and CIGS tandem thin-film solar cells. Research explores alternatives to ultrasonic spin-coating for conformal deposition at industrial scale, alongside techniques for pinhole effect reduction.
